Today, [Anouk van Kasteren](https://anoukvankasteren.com/) presented our CHI 2026 poster ['Now What?' Modelling Route Choices for Personalised Context-aware Decision Support During Public Transport Disruptions](/publications/vankasteren-2026-nowwhat) in Barcelona, Spain.

This work investigates how context influences travelers' route choices when services fail and explores how decision-support systems can better assist travelers in these situations.

Also, stay tuned for our [follow-up study](/publications/vankasteren-2026-modelling) that will be published at UMAP 2026 later this year.

I am very happy about this collaboration with [Anouk van Kasteren](https://anoukvankasteren.com/), [Marloes Vredenborg](https://marloesvredenborg.com/), and [Judith Masthoff](https://www.uu.nl/staff/JFMMasthoff).
