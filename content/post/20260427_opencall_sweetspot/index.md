As part of Open Garden 2026, composers and sound artists are invited to submit concepts for a sound installation engaging with the theme *Territory*.

Four concepts will be selected to be presented as sound installations in June 2026 in the Mirabell Garden in Salzburg, Austria, as part of the [SweetSpot---Lounge for Electroacoustic Music](https://w-k.sbg.ac.at/forschungsthema/sweet-spot/) series.
The compositions will be heard during the day (18--25 June 2026) as a permanent installation via a 16-channel speaker system integrated into the garden architecture of the Mirabell Garden.

**Deadline: 8 May 2026**

For details (in German and English), see full [call for concepts as a pdf](call_garden_2026.pdf).
