I am still reflecting on the first [**AlpCHI Conference**](/events/alpchi2026), which took place on the beautiful Monte Verità near Ascona, Switzerland.

Co-organizing this inaugural edition was a real privilege. Our goal was to create a small and welcoming space for thoughtful exchange within the HCI community. It was wonderful to see this vision come to life through inspiring talks and engaging discussions throughout the conference.

We were fortunate to have four outstanding keynote speakers—  
[**Yvonne Rogers**](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/33314-yvonne-rogers),  
[**Elisabeth André**](https://www.uni-augsburg.de/en/fakultaet/fai/informatik/prof/hcm/team/andre/),  
[**David Kim**](https://davidkim.de), and  
[**Harald Reiterer**](https://hci.uni-konstanz.de/members/prof-dr-harald-reiterer/)—   
whose talks sparked many interesting discussions.

Many thanks to all participants for contributing to such an open and engaging atmosphere!

I am also very grateful to the fantastic organizing team:

- [Tiare Feuchtner](https://hci.uni-konstanz.de/members/feuchtner/) (University of Konstanz)  
- [Michael Haller](https://www.mediainteractionlab.eu/?page_id=561) (Free University of Bozen-Bolzano)  
- [Denis Lalanne](https://lalanne.human-ist.ch) (University of Fribourg)  
- [Johannes Schöning](https://johannesschoening.de/) (University of St. Gallen)  
- [Verena Zimmermann](https://spg.ethz.ch/group/people/professor.html) (ETH Zürich)  
- [Anke Reinschluessel](https://hci.uni-konstanz.de/members/research-assistants/anke-reinschluessel/) (University of Konstanz)  
- [Alexandre De Masi](https://ademasi.ch/) (University of Geneva)  
- [Jannis Strecker-Bischoff](https://academia.jrstrecker.de/) (University of St. Gallen)

It was a pleasure working with all of you.

Special thanks also to the wonderful team at [Monte Verità](https://www.monteverita.org) and [Congressi Stefano Franscini](https://csf.ethz.ch) for hosting us so beautifully.

I am already looking forward to the next edition of **[AlpCHI](https://alpchi.org/) in 2028 in Konstanz**.
