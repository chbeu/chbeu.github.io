I had the honor contributing to a feature article in [Arbeit & Wirtschaft (issue 2/2026, pp. 40-43, German)](https://www.arbeit-wirtschaft.at/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/AW_02_2026_Gesundheit_Web.pdf) on how generative AI (GenAI) is changing the music landscape. 

The music industry has always been shaped by technological innovations. But with GenAI, the value chain is shifting even more away from artists and moves toward platforms and technology providers.
