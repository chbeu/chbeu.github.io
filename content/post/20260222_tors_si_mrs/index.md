Together with [Elena V. Epure](https://elenavepure.faculty.bio), [Andrés Ferraro](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=WQglBowAAAAJ&hl=en), and [Lorenzo Porcaro](https://lorenzoporcaro.me), I am serving as a Guest Editor for a Special Issue of [ACM Transactions on Recommender Systems](https://dl.acm.org/journal/tors/) on 'Music Recommender Systems'.

The [Call for Papers](TORS_CfP_SI_MRS.pdf) and the submission details are already available.

- Abstract deadline: 7 May 2026
- Submission deadline: ***14 May 2026***
- First-round review decisions: July 2026
- Deadline for revision submissions: September 2026
- Notification of final decisions: December 2026

We are looking forward to your submissions.
